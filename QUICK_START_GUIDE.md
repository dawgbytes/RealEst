## ‚úÖ PROPERTY CRITERIA CHECKLIST

- [ ] **NOT under contract** (check MLS status)

- [ ] **ARV is 1.5x+ purchase price** (for $40k+ profit)
- [ ] **Active listing** (not expired, not pending)
- [ ] **Quick close possible** (7-14 days)
- [ ] **Strong comparable sales** (3+ recent sales nearby)
- [ ] **High-demand area** (quick resale potential)
