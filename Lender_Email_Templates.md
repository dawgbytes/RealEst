## EMAIL BEST PRACTICES

### Before Sending:
1. **Personalize:** Replace all bracketed placeholders with your actual information
2. **Research:** Mention something specific about the lender (their website, a program they offer, etc.)
3. **Proofread:** Check for spelling and grammar errors
4. **Attachments:** Only attach relevant documents (property details, not personal financial info in initial email)

### Follow-Up Strategy:
- Send initial email
- Follow up in 3-5 business days if no response
- Call if email doesn't get response after 1 week
- Be persistent but professional

### What to Include in Attachments (When Requested):
- Property listing/details
- Comparable sales analysis
- Purchase contract (if under contract)
- Property photos
- Basic financial summary

### What NOT to Include in Initial Email:
- Social Security Number
- Bank statements
- Tax returns
- Credit reports
- Personal financial details (save for application)
